Pork Tenderloin

  • Ingredients
  • Description
  • Directions

Ingredients

Oil 1 cup
Soy sauce 1/2 cup
Red wine vinegar 1 tablespoon
Honey 2 tablespoons
Brown sugar 2 tablespoons
Minced garlic 1 tablespoon

Directions

  1. Combine marinade ingredients together in a large bowl and stir.
  2. Remove tenderloin from package and remove excess fat from cut.
  3. Tenderize the meat by poking holes throughout with a fork. This also increases marinade absorption.
  4. Transfer marinade and pork to ziploc bag. Let marinate for 2+ hours.
  5. Once marinated, throw tenderloin on a hot grill. Grill 6 minutes a side.
  6. Serve.
